About Us


Terracore Solutions is a full-service real estate and property company engaged in the sale and development of real estate, offering houses for rent and sale, offices and commercial shops for lease, and the supply of quality luxury building materials including plumbing pipes,quality foriegn doors, toilets, and related products. We also provide top-grade professional land services such as land surveying, land uplift, beacon placement, and allied works.

Who we are

Terracore Solutions is a member-drive organisation focused on property investment, development and building materials supply. For years we've helped families and investors secure, develop and manage valuable real estate with integrity.

Our mission

To provide transparent, professional and affordable property and construction solutions that empower members and improve communities.

Our values

  • Trust: We work with full transparency and accountability.
  • Quality: We deliver durable solutions and excellent service.
  • Community: We prioritise local impact and member welfare.
